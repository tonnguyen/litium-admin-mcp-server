# Payment extensions

Payment service providers (PSP) supported by Litium.

For Litium 8, some payment providers are [available as Litium Apps](/apps/overview). Please note, however, that some add-ons are not available in Litium 8 yet. [Consult our roadmap to find out what's prioritized next for Litium 8](https://www.litium.com/roadmap), or [contact Litium support with any further questions](/cloud/serverless/overview#support).

For Litium 7, you can also build a payment provider plugin for your solution. This is not available for Litium 8.

## Available Payment Providers

The following payment providers are available as extensions:

- [Adyen](/apps/adyen-payment/overview)
- [Briqpay](/apps/briqpay-payment/overview)
- DirectPay
- [Klarna](/apps/klarna-payment/overview)
- Nets
- PayPal
- [Qliro](/apps/qliro-payment/overview)
- [Svea Checkout](/apps/svea-payment/overview)
- SwedbankPay

<Note>
Some payment providers are being migrated to Litium Apps for Litium 8. Check the individual provider documentation for availability and migration guides.
</Note>
